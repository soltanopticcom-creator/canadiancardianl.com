About Canadian Cardinal

Canadian Cardinal is an independent eyewear label based in Montréal, Quebec. We design focused collections of sunglasses and optical frames for international buyers and distributors. Our frames blend clean lines, everyday comfort, and reliable build quality—made to perform in daily use without shouting for attention.

From our studio in Griffintown, Montréal, we work directly with partners worldwide to deliver curated assortments, consistent QC, and responsive communication. Materials and finishes are selected with care, balancing weight, durability, and a smooth wearing experience.

  • UV400 and optional polarized lenses
  • Acetate and stainless-steel (or titanium) constructions
  • Consistent fit testing across core sizes
  • Controlled color and finish standards
  • MOQ-friendly production windows for B2B
Location: Griffintown, Montréal, QC, Canada
